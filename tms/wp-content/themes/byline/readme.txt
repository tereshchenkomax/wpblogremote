== ABOUT BYLINE ==

Simplify your site with Byline, a theme specifically built for writers. Display your latest article in its entirety, including comments, right on your home page. Looks great on desktop browsers, tablets and handheld devices. For a live demo go to http://demos.alphabetthemes.com/?theme=byline.
